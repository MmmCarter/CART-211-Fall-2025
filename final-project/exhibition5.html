User Behavior Prediction Algorithms

User behavior-based prediction algorithms utilize behavioral data collected from users on the platform—such as clicks, views, purchases, likes, comments, and dwell time—to forecast future actions or preferences.

Netflix、YouTube、Spotify: Predict content or products users will like.

Google Ads、Facebook Ads: Predict click-through rates (CTR) and conversion rates to optimize ad placements.

Amazon、eBay: Predict Purchase Intent and Repurchase Rate.
