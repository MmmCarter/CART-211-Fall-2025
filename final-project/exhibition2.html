THE LISTENING HOUSE

Do You Know?

Reports indicate that smart speakers may be implanted with Trojan programs. Attackers can record audio without the device being activated and upload the recorded voice data to the cloud.

Smart home appliances increasingly utilize sensors—such as thermal sensors and cameras—to recognize their surroundings, interpret actions, and make decisions.

Information collected by sensors may reconstruct users' daily behaviors, forming a “behavioral profile.” Attackers may exploit vulnerabilities to gain control over smart cameras, enabling real-time surveillance or the theft of video content.
