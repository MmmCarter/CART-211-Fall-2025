EMOTIONAL TARGETING

If the platform detects that a user:

Anxiety → Promote stress-relief products and mental health services

Loneliness → Promote social networking apps and food delivery services

Anger → Promote political content or radical viewpoints

Happiness → Stimulate shopping desire by promoting luxury goods or entertainment content

This is one of the most important business models for surveillance capitalism. Advertising doesn't just sell products; it sells “emotional solutions.”
